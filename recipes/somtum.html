Somtum Recipe

Description

Som Tum, also known as green papaya salad, is a spicy and tangy Thai salad. It is made with shredded unripe papaya, tomatoes, green beans, peanuts, and sometimes shrimp or salted crab. The salad is typically dressed with a mixture of lime juice, fish sauce, palm sugar, and chili peppers. The flavors of Som Tum are a balance of sour, spicy, salty, and slightly sweet. It is often enjoyed as a refreshing and zesty appetizer or side dish.

Steps

  • Peel and shred unripe papaya.
  • In a mortar and pestle, pound garlic, chili peppers, and palm sugar.
  • Add shredded papaya, cherry tomatoes, green beans, and peanuts to the mortar.
  • Crush and mix the ingredients together, gradually adding lime juice and fish sauce.
  • Adjust the flavors to achieve a balance of sour, spicy, and salty.
  • Serve topped with additional peanuts and garnish with fresh herbs.
