Lasagna Recipe

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with stacked layers of pasta sheets, meat sauce, cheese, and often, additional ingredients like vegetables or mushrooms. The pasta sheets are typically layered with a meaty tomato sauce (bolognese), ricotta or béchamel sauce, and a variety of cheeses such as mozzarella and Parmesan. The layers are baked until the cheese is melted and bubbly, creating a rich and comforting casserole. Lasagna is a hearty and satisfying dish that is enjoyed by many around the world.

Steps

  1. Cook lasagna sheets according to package instructions.
  2. Prepare a meaty tomato sauce (bolognese) by browning ground beef with onions and garlic, then adding tomato sauce and herbs.
  3. Layer the lasagna sheets in a baking dish, alternating with the bolognese sauce and a mixture of ricotta or béchamel sauce.
  4. Sprinkle grated mozzarella and Parmesan cheese between the layers.
  5. Bake in the oven until the cheese is melted and bubbly, and the lasagna is cooked through.
  6. Let it rest for a few minutes before serving.
