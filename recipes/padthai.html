Padthai Recipe

Description

Pad Thai is a popular Thai stir-fried noodle dish. It typically consists of rice noodles stir-fried with tofu, shrimp, or chicken, along with bean sprouts, eggs, and various seasonings. The dish is often flavored with tamarind paste, fish sauce, lime juice, and crushed peanuts. Pad Thai is known for its combination of sweet, sour, and savory flavors, and it is commonly garnished with fresh herbs and lime wedges.

Steps

  1. Soak rice noodles in warm water until softened.

  3. Add garlic, shallots, and beaten eggs to the pan.
  4. Stir in drained noodles, along with fish sauce, tamarind paste, sugar, and lime juice.
  5. Toss in bean sprouts and chopped green onions.
  6. Serve garnished with crushed peanuts, lime wedges, and fresh herbs.
